A new mobile experience for IU.

University users expect native mobile applications, not just progressive web apps or responsive web pages. We want to do things on our phones that are fully featured, easy to use, and easy to find. We expect these apps in the Apple and Google app stores. We are addressing these expectations by replacing the current application named “IU Mobile” with a new application cleverly named “IU Mobile.” It will focus on delivering a more holistic experience and deliver unique experiences to users of different campuses and affiliation types.

The beta release

The IU Mobile application will be created, maintained, and improved through a
partnership between IU Studios and UITS. The base feature set will be available to
beta testers early 2020.

Improving the application

The early focus is making IU Mobile support broad, high-impact features, that are useful for the largest groups of IU affiliates. We’re focused on data about campus and services.
